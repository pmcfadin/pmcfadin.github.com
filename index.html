About Me

Technologist, Speaker, and Open Source Advocate

With over 15 years as an Oracle DBA/Developer and extensive experience as Chief Architect at Hobsons, I've dedicated my career to solving complex distributed systems challenges. As Chief Evangelist for Apache Cassandra at DataStax, I help organizations build some of the largest and most exciting deployments in production.

I co-authored "Managing Cloud Native Data on Kubernetes" for O'Reilly and travel the world educating developers about distributed databases, streaming systems, and the future of data infrastructure. When I'm not working with Cassandra, Pulsar, Spark, or Flink, you'll find me exploring retro computing or capturing the cosmos through astrophotography.
